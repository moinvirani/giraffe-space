About Giraffe Space

Giraffe Space is a gamified mobile app designed to help you learn and practice Nonviolent Communication (NVC), a powerful framework for compassionate communication developed by Dr. Marshall Rosenberg.

Like Duolingo for emotional intelligence, Giraffe Space makes learning NVC engaging and accessible through daily practice exercises, guided journaling, and AI-assisted conversation practice.

Key Features

  • Interactive NVC Course: 4 foundational lessons covering observations, feelings, needs, and requests
  • Daily Practice: Gamified exercises including "Spot the Judgment," "Feeling or Faux," and scenario practice
  • Giraffe Journal:strong> Guided journaling templates to process real-life situations using NVC
  • Gigi AI Assistant: Practice conversations and get NVC guidance from our AI companion
  • Progress Tracking: Streaks, XP rewards, and level progression to keep you motivated
  • NVC Vocabulary:strong> Comprehensive reference for feelings, needs, and faux feelings

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nonviolent Communication (NVC)?

NVC is a communication framework developed by Dr. Marshall Rosenberg that focuses on expressing observations, feelings, needs, and requests clearly while listening with empathy. The "giraffe" symbolizes compassionate communication (long neck for perspective, big heart for empathy).

div>

Is Giraffe Space free to use?

Yes! Giraffe Space offers a free tier that includes the introduction course, daily practice exercises, and 10 AI chat messages per day. Premium subscribers get unlimited AI chats, advanced exercises, unlimited journaling, and an ad-free experience.

div>

How much does Premium cost?

Premium is available as a monthly subscription ($4.99/month) or annual subscription ($29.99/year, saving 50%).

div>

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel, go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iOS device, find Giraffe Space, and tap Cancel Subscription.

div>

Is the Gigi AI a replacement for therapy?

No. Gigi is an AI assistant designed to help you practice NVC concepts. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing mental health challenges, please consult a qualified mental health professional.

div>

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open the app and go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase Premium.

div>

What age is this app appropriate for?

Giraffe Space is designed for users 12 and older. The content focuses on emotional intelligence and communication skills suitable for teens and adults.
