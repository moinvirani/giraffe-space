## 6. 13-inch iPad Screenshots

**Location:** Main page ‚Üí Previews and Screenshots ‚Üí 13" Display

**Required sizes for 13-inch iPad:**
- 2064 x 2752 pixels (portrait)
- 2752 x 2064 pixels (landscape)

**Options:**
1. **Use iPhone screenshots scaled:** App Store Connect sometimes accepts this
2. **Generate iPad screenshots:** Use your existing iPhone screenshots and resize them
3. **Run on iPad Simulator:** Take screenshots directly

**Quick Solution:** If you don't have iPad screenshots, you can:
- Use the same screenshots from iPhone 6.7" display
- Or check "This app is designed for iPhone only" in App Information if you don't want to support iPad
