## What is Giraffe Space?

Giraffe Space is a gamified mobile app that teaches **Nonviolent Communication (NVC)** ‚Äî a powerful framework developed by Dr. Marshall Rosenberg for connecting with yourself and others through compassion rather than judgment.

In NVC, the **giraffe** (with its long neck for perspective and big heart) represents compassionate communication, while the **jackal** represents our reactive, judgmental tendencies. The app helps you transform "jackal thoughts" into "giraffe speak."

## The Four Components of NVC

| # | Component | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Observations** | What we see/hear without evaluation |
| 2 | **Feelings** | Our emotional response (not thoughts about others) |
| 3 | **Needs** | Universal human needs underlying feelings |
| 4 | **Requests** | Clear, doable actions we'd like |
